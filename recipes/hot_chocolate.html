Hot Chocolate

Description

When the weather outside is looking frightful, cozy up with some hot chocolate to beat the chill. This classic drink is guaranteed to warm you up and bring back some childhood memories! This simple recipe will have you whipping up some hot chocolate in no time at all.

Ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Half-and-half

Directions:

  1. Pour water into a pot, bring to a boil.
  2. Add dry ingredients and whisk in the boiling water.
  3. Bring to a simmer and cook, stirring constantly for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in milk, remove from heat.
  5. Stir in vanilla
  6. Pour hot chocolate into mugs and finish off with half-and-half.
