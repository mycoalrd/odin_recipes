Cereal

Description

Sometimes you're tired and don't want to make anything, but you got to eat something. A quick 30 minute meal seems the way to go, but you're not up for the task of cooking. We've all been there. That's where ole reliable comes in to save the day! A bowl of cereal is a classic staple that always comes in clutch when you want to have a bite to eat and don't want to put in much effort. This "recipe" will have you chowing down in no time at all.

Make sure to follow the directions carefully because if you reverse the order then that would make you a weirdo !

Ingredients:

  • Cereal
  • Milk
  • Optional: fruit (preferably bananas, blueberries, or strawberries)

Directions:

  1. Pour your favorite brand of cereal into a bowl. Use your own discretion for amount. If you want to add fruit, now is the time to cut it up and place atop of cereal.
  2. Pour your choice of milk or milk substitute into bowl.
